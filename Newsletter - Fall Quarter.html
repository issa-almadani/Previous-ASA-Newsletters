All of Us Research Program


The University of Chicago's Institute for Population and Precision Health is looking for research participants for the All of Us Research Program - "a longitudional, population based research project funded by the National Institute of Health". The goal of the project is to gather health data from individuals not commonly represented in other resaerch studies, with the aim of producing a comprehensible and diverse database pertaining to various peoples and diseases.

Participants will receive compensation of $25 and can receive further compensation by also participating in the "University of Chicago's population-based reasearch program, COMPASS".

Please contact researcher Victoria Hwang at victoriah@bsd.uchicago.edu or via phone at 773-795-4767 for more information about this opportunity!

Leader's of Color - Institute of Politics

The LoC is an organization at UChicago's IOP that prepares students of color for leadership positions and careers through leadership development, community building, mentorship, site visits, and various other activities. They are one of the largest networks of students of color at UChicago, and have reached out to the ASA, hoping to expand their representation to encompass more comminuties. Applications are due by the end of the fall quarter, and we will be providing further information in upcoming newsletters as the deadline grows nearer.
