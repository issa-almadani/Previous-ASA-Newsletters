Winter Formal!

IT'S HAPPENING GUYS! The ASA is excited to officially announce that the inaugural annual Winter Formal, Tura'thuna (تراثنا - translating to "Our Heritage"), will be held on Saturday, March 5th from 6:30 to 9:30 PM! Join us at the Cloister Club in Ida Noyes Hall for dabke, oud performances, mashawi, and much more.
Dress Code: Traditional Arab or Formal Attire. Check the timeline below to get a look into what you can expect at the formal!
Ticket sale ends March 1st @ Noon - get yours while you still can as seats are limited!

LGBTQ+ Series

The ASA is excited to announce that we will be launching a new series highlighting queerness in the Arab world. We're currently in the process of planning a gender and sexuality discussion for the first part of our series. More information coming soon. All suggestions for events you'd like to see are welcome!

Things to Look Forward to for Spring Quarter

The ASA is currently planning many activities for the spring quarter, including a trip to the Art Institute followed by the Starbucks Reserve, a Knafe Karoake night, and spring festival... as well as any recommendations you might have!
