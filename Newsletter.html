Camp Alima

"Camp Alima is a student-led organization fostering the advancement of skills relating to science, technology, engineering, math, and leadership among high-school aged Moroccan girls. In a week-long summer camp in Morocco, 20 students will be exposed to an interactive and rigorous curriculum consisting of experiments relating to the STEM fields such as biology and computer science as well as a leadership development curriculum." Camp Alima is now looking to expand their team and are recruiting for general membership and leadership positions. Please check the flyer attached above for more details, and feel free to reach out to Nissma Bencheikh, the Executive Director at Camp Alima, if you have any questions about the experience (nissmaben@berkeley.edu). Application deadline is February 11th at 11:59 PM PST!
